# Product principles

Distilled from the master plan (§3). These guide every implementation phase.

1. **Open-source first.** Inspectable, runnable and forkable from day one. The app demonstrates the same standards it asks users to apply to their repositories.
2. **Useful in under five minutes.** No database, no cloud account, no OAuth dance, no Docker. Enter a username, see findings.
3. **Local-first by default.** Public repository analysis runs on the user's machine. Nothing about the user or their repositories is uploaded.
4. **AI-compatible, not AI-dependent.** The deterministic core must remain valuable on its own. AI is opt-in and additive — never required.
5. **Transparent scoring.** No mystery scores. Every score lists what was checked, what passed, what failed and what was not applicable.
6. **Helpful, not judgmental.** RepoPulse is a coach, not a linter. Suggestions are calm, constructive and prioritized.
7. **General health first, job-market mode later.** Documentation, presentation and maintainability come before role-specific or recruiter-facing features.
8. **Designed for many personas.** Students, juniors, seniors, maintainers, teachers and small teams should all get value without friction.
9. **Presentation matters.** RepoPulse should itself feel portfolio-ready.
